Meet the Developers!



Who we are:

We are a group of computer scientists from Georgia Southern University, based in Savannah, Georgia. Our group members consist of Katelyn Crotts, Brett Mitchell, Wesley Stoneman, and Jonathan Zheng.


Our purpose:

We began developing this project from Joseph Weizenbaum's original idea of the Eliza program. Our goal was to branch out from its basic "therapuetic" functionality, and take a more "laugh therapy" approach. Starting with yo momma jokes, the expanded eliza bot will return a witty remark with each request. Our project is still in its infant stage, with huge capabilities of expansion.
